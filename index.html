Controlled-access • Packaging-level safety

Lock medication access between doses—without apps, screens, or behavior change.

FailSafe is a discreet, locking pill bottle cap that unlocks only at scheduled dosing times and automatically re-locks after use, helping reduce misuse, diversion, accidental overdose, and pediatric access.

Request Information Who it’s for
Early-stage venture. Interested in pilots, partnerships, or packaging integration—reach out.

The Problem

Once prescription medications enter the home, physical access is largely unrestricted. Traditional child-resistant caps and reminder-based “smart caps” do not prevent early, duplicate, or unauthorized access after the bottle is opened.

The Solution

FailSafe adds physical access control at the packaging level: a familiar pharmacy-cap experience with an internal timed lock that permits access only at prescribed intervals.

Unlocks only at dosing times

Prevents early or duplicate dosing by controlling access between scheduled intervals.

Automatically re-locks

Locks again when closed, reducing the risk of repeated access and diversion.

No app required

No screens, tracking, or behavior change—designed to be passive and non-intrusive.

Designed For

Pharmaceutical manufacturers, pharmacies, healthcare systems, and vulnerable patient populations where safety, autonomy, and adherence matter.

Use cases

  • Controlled substances (Schedule II–IV): reduce household diversion and impulsive overuse.
  • Pediatric safety: an added layer of protection beyond standard child-resistant closures.
  • Dementia/Alzheimer’s: helps prevent accidental double-dosing with a familiar user experience.
  • Veterans & active-duty military: supports adherence without surveillance or apps.
