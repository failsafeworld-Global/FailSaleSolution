Controlled-access packaging • Safety between doses

Access control, built into the cap.

FailSafe is a controlled-access pharmaceutical safety cap designed to limit physical access to prescription medications between scheduled dosing times—helping reduce misuse, diversion, accidental overdose, and pediatric exposure.

How it works

A familiar pharmacy-cap experience—designed to be passive, discreet, and protective.

Unlocks at scheduled intervals

Access is permitted only during the prescribed window—reducing early or duplicate dosing.

Automatically re-locks

Once closed, the cap returns to a locked state, helping reduce repeated access and diversion.

No app required

No screens, pairing, tracking, or behavior change. Packaging-level safety that “just works.”

Designed for

Use cases where safety, autonomy, and adherence matter—without surveillance or complexity.

Primary use cases

  • Controlled substances: reduce household diversion and impulsive overuse.
  • Pediatric safety: adds protection beyond standard child-resistant closures.
  • Dementia/Alzheimer’s: helps prevent accidental double dosing with a familiar interface.
  • High-risk settings: supports adherence without requiring apps or monitoring.
