FailSafe

Controlled-access pharmaceutical safety cap for prescription medications.

The Problem

Once prescription medications enter the home, physical access is largely unrestricted. This contributes to misuse, diversion, accidental overdose, and pediatric exposure— especially with controlled substances.

The Solution

FailSafe is a discreet, locking pill bottle cap that unlocks only at scheduled dosing times and automatically re-locks after use. It functions like a standard pharmacy cap and requires no apps, screens, or behavior change.

Designed For

Pharmaceutical manufacturers, pharmacies, healthcare systems, and vulnerable patient populations where safety, autonomy, and adherence matter.
